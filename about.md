# About Me

My name is Advaith Menon John

I build technical systems at the intersection of aerospace, robotics, and machine learning. My work focuses on solving hard engineering problems with structured thinking, strong data discipline, and real implementation.

I am especially interested in space systems, autonomous robotics, and signal validation. Rather than just experimenting, I design full pipelines from preprocessing and modeling to evaluation and deployment. I care about measurable results, not just ideas.

Most of my projects center on three areas:
- Aerospace engineering and space systems  
- Robotics and autonomous control  
- Machine learning for real world signal analysis  

I approach projects like engineering problems, not school assignments. That means defining the objective clearly, building modular systems, testing assumptions, and improving performance based on data.

My long term goal is to build advanced aerospace and robotics systems that operate at scale. Everything I work on now is preparation for that level.

If you are interested in collaboration, research, or technical discussion, feel free to reach out.
